Subtitle Sidekick

Keep subtitles in sight, without letting them get in the way of the movie!

How To Use

  1. Upload a .SRT file in the Media tab, or drag the file onto the page.
  2. Place your laptop near your TV or viewing area.
  3. Start the movie and press Play.
  4. Use controls in the Media tab to synchronize

Hotkeys

Space Play / Pause
Left/Right Adjust offset
F Toggle fullscreen
H Show / hide menu
